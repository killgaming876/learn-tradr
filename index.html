Lesson 1: Understanding Candlesticks

Candlesticks are the building blocks of price action analysis. Each candle tells a story about the battle between buyers and sellers.

Key Concepts:

  • Body: Shows opening and closing prices
  • Wicks/Shadows: Show highest and lowest prices
  • Green/White: Price closed higher than it opened
  • Red/Black: Price closed lower than it opened

Look at the practice chart and try to identify different candle types. Long bodies show strong conviction, while long wicks show rejection.
